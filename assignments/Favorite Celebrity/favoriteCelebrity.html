Beginnings

Turner's musical journey began at a young age as he grew up in a household of music enthusiasts.Turner's entry into the world of rock music came with the Strokes' breakthrough in 2001 when he was only 15.

In 2002 Turner and some friends decied to form their own rock bandArctic Monkeys,they rehearsed for a year in Turner's garage before they had their debut live performance.

Career

2005-2007 was their rise to fame,Arctic Monkeys gained national attention with Turner being the lead singer.

Their single "I Bet You Look Good on the Dancefloor" debuted at number one on the UK Singles Chart,and also their album "Whatever people say I am,That's what I'm not" debuted at number one on the UK Albums Chart.
